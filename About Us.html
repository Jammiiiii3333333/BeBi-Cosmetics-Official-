A B O U T   B E B I

Catering to budding makeup artists, this blog specializes in advocating for morena makeup and Filipino-friendly beauty products, offering invaluable advice and tutorials specifically tailored to the needs of young and novice individuals venturing into the world of makeup artistry. With a focus on inclusivity, it highlights techniques and product recommendations that embrace the diverse spectrum of the Filipino skin, empowering beginners to explore and celebrate their unique beauty.

Why Bebi?
